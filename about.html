Since I was a young child I developed a interest for the arts, particularly drawing which led me to study Fine Arts with a minor in Graphic Design in my home country.

After graduating I worked primarily in the printing industry as a graphic designer. Some years after, the oportunity to move to Canada showed up. It was in this country that I realized I needed to gain a fresh perspective on the design industry, so I enrolled in the Interactive Media Design Program at Allgonquin College.

After school, I hope to succeed as a freelancer and continue to do the things I love.
